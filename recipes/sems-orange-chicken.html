Sem's Orange Chicken

Description

If you want to brighten up the dinner, a side of orange chicken is a no brainer. As the name suggests, this dish would add a world of color to eliminate any monochromacy from the dinner table. With the mix of spice and sweetness, this is a guaranteed winner for your tastebuds. So, try this recipe if you haven't done so yet, you won't regret it!

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp of corn starch
  • 3 tbsp of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of orange juice
  • 2 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tsp of vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Paprika
  • Salt (as per taste)
  • Sugar (as per taste)

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into small cubes.
  2. Make the mixture for marinating: add 4 tbsp corn starch, 3 tbsp flour, salt, 1 egg, seasoning (ig: paprika), garlic, ginger. Mix it with hand.
  3. Add chicken to the mix.
  4. Wait for an hour.
  5. Cook in oil (wait until bubbling).
  6. Make the sauce: add 1 cup of orange juice, 2 tbsp of soy sauce, 1 tsp of vinegar, 1 tbsp of corn starch. Mix it with wisk.
  7. Turn the heat on (6-7 minutes). Keep whisking while its on the heat.
  8. Add 2 tbsp of sugar (according to taste).
  9. Add chicken on sauce.
  10. Mix well and serve.

Enjoy your meal!
