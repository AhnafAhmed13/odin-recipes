Dhakan's Delicious Dish

Knorr Noodles

Description

Dive into the world of deliciousness with Dhakan's delicious dish recipe. It's fork licking good!

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 packets of Knorr noodles
  • 2 seekh kebabs (or any other meat)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 whole cucumber
  • 20 grams of mayonnaise
  • 10 grams of ketchup
  • Soy sauce
  • Spices (as per taste)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Water for Noodles: Take the spice from the Knorr noodles packets and mix it into the water. Put it onto the stove and bring it to a boil.
  2. Cook the Meat: While the water is heating, put your seekh kebabs or other meat for cooking. The seekh kebab itself is very flavorful and adds spice.
  3. Cook the Noodles: Once the water boils, add the Knorr noodles and cook as per the packet instructions.
  4. Fry the Egg (Optional): If desired, fry an egg to add extra yum yum to the dish.
  5. Prepare the Cucumber Sauce:
    • Shred a whole cucumber with a cheese grater.
    • Mix the shredded cucumber with some soy sauce.
  6. Prepare the Mayo-Ketchup Mix:
    • Mix 20 grams of mayo with 10 grams of ketchup.
    • Combine it with the cucumber and soy sauce mixture.
  7. Combine All Ingredients: Mix the noodles, meat, fried egg (if using), and mayo-ketchup-cucumber sauce together.
  8. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the delicious dish hot and enjoy the mix of flavors.

Notes:

  • The seekh kebab is quite flavorful, and you can adjust the spices according to your preference.
  • Feel free to experiment with other meats or add additional vegetables for a customized dish.

Enjoy your meal!
